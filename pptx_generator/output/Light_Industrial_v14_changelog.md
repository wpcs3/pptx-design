## Phase 5: Writing Style Improvements

### Voice Consistency
- **Third-person** for market analysis: "Industrial real estate demonstrates...", "The 130 bps cap rate expansion reflects..."
- **First-person plural** for strategy/GP content: "Our acquisition criteria include...", "We target properties with..."

### Language Tightening
- "highest among all CRE property types" → "highest among property types"
- "structural undersupply in the <50,000 SF segment" → "structural undersupply in the sub-50,000 SF segment"
- Varied repetitive phrases: "construction pipeline at decade lows" → alternated with "minimal new supply", "constrained development"
- "mark-to-market opportunity" → varied with "rent upside", "embedded value", "below-market leases"

### Forward-Looking Framing
- Added: "Our base case projects...", "We expect...", "Forward indicators suggest..."
- Strengthened future-oriented language in returns and outlook sections
